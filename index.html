Your path to Mindful Productivity

In a world of endless to-do lists, Trio helps you cut through the noise. Focus on just three key tasks daily to drive meaningful progress and maintain balance in your life.

Why Trio Works for You

  • Simplify Your Day

    Trio limits you to three essential tasks each day, cutting through the clutter and helping you focus on what truly matters.

  • Cultivate Mindful Progress

    With smart daily planning reminders and evening reflections, Trio encourages intentional goal-setting and celebrates your achievements, fostering a sense of accomplishment.

  • Balance Productivity and Wellbeing

    Trio's gentle approach reminds you to take breaks, preventing burnout while ensuring consistent, long-term progress towards your goals.

Features

  • Intelligent Notifications

    Thoughtfully timed morning and evening prompts encourage deliberate planning and reflection, promoting sustained productivity while safeguarding against burnout.

  • At-a-Glance Task Management

    Trio's home screen widget provides immediate access to your daily priorities, allowing for seamless task updates without disrupting your workflow.

  • Aesthetic Customization

    Tailor the app's appearance to your preferences with a selection of color themes and icons, creating a personalized productivity environment.
